About Me

I'm an audio engineer and musician specializing in music post-production for film and television.

With a background spanning recording, editing, and mixing across multiple genres, I focus on delivering polished, emotionally impactful sound that supports story.

Recent credits include major studio productions such as How to Train Your Dragon (Universal Pictures), Wicked and Wicked: For Good (Universal Pictures), and Better Man (Paramount Pictures).

Whether on large-scale features or detailed music editorial, I bring a musician's sensitivity and an engineer's precision to every production.
