Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Wall Countdown Timer (“the App”) is a full-screen countdown timer for iPhone and iPad. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

Information we collect

The App does not collect personal information from you and does not send your data to any server.

Countdown settings you enter (such as titles and target dates) are stored locally on your device using app storage (UserDefaults).

How we use information

Locally stored countdown settings are used only to display your countdown timer on your device.

Sharing of information

We do not share your data with third parties because the App does not transmit your data off your device.

Data retention

Your countdown settings remain stored on your device. You can edit or remove them anytime from within the App.

Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children. If you believe a child has used the App and you have concerns, please contact us so we can address them.
